Strategic Pathways | Where Global Skills Come Full Circle

Empowering the Kenyan Diaspora and Study-Abroad Returnees through Brain Circulation

Strategic Pathways is a Kenya-based digital-enabled platform designed to mobilise thousands of study-abroad returnees and diaspora professionals. We transform global exposure into measurable local impact by connecting skilled Kenyans with locally grounded economic and development opportunities.

The Problem: Brain Drain vs Brain Circulation

Each year, thousands of Kenyans gain advanced education and professional experience abroad. However, they often encounter fragmented networks and limited structured collaboration pathways upon returning. This contributes to brain drain. Strategic Pathways converts these globally acquired skills into structured local economic opportunities.

Our Proposed Solution: A Talent Execution and Venture-Building Platform

Rather than functioning merely as a job board or social network, Strategic Pathways operates as a talent execution platform. We curate skilled professionals, assemble cross-functional teams, and deploy them into income-generating, development-aligned projects, consultancy engagements, and venture-building initiatives.

Who We Serve

  • Study-Abroad Returnees: Professionals seeking structured engagement and productive deployment of their global skills.
  • Diaspora Professionals: Individuals wanting to contribute to Kenya's growth from abroad.
  • Institutions (Counties, NGOs, SMEs): Organizations struggling to find reliable, execution-ready talent for high-quality projects.
  • Donors & Development Partners: Seeking measurable local capacity building and sustainable venture development.

Our Long-Term Vision

We aim to evolve into a national talent infrastructure platform, a cross-county execution network, and a robust venture-building ecosystem. We focus on youth employment, SME development, and local capacity building to drive sustainable economic growth objectives in Kenya.
